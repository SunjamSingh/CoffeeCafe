About Us

"A bad day with coffee is better than a day without it."


In December 2005, the first Coffee Café opened its doors in the bucktown neighbourhood in Chicago USA. Made from the most exemplary coffee beans grown in the lush green plantations of Coorg, Coffee Café has been serving the only the finest Arabica to its customers for the last 15 years. Over the years, Coffee Café has earned a place in the hearts of coffee lovers all over Chicago. What started as a small family owned coffee bar in Chicago, is today one of the best places for coffee lovers to hang out.

Coffee Café aims to reciprocate the love that it receives for its customers by giving back to the community. The Coffee Café Foundation funds many organisations in Illinois working to make the world a better place.
